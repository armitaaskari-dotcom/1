My Three Favorite Colors

The Psychology of Colors

Pink

Pink is often associated with qualities like compassion, nurturing, and calmness. It’s seen as a soothing
and gentle color, often evoking feelings of love and warmth. It can also be playful and fun, depending
on the shade.

White

The color white is often associated with purity, simplicity, and cleanliness. It tends to create
a sense of calm and space, and it can help other colors stand out. White is also linked to
new beginnings and is often used to convey a modern, minimalist aesthetic. It pairs well with
almost any color, making it super versatile in design.

Black

Black is often associated with elegance, sophistication, and power. It can convey a sense of formality
and timelessness. Black is also linked to mystery and depth, and it can create a striking contrast
when paired with lighter colors. It’s often used to evoke sophistication and can add a modern and
sleek look to designs.
